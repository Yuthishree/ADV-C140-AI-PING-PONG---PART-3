AI PING PONG GAME







Instructions

  1. Make sure you are at least 2 feet away from the laptop and that your webcam is on,
    if not click allow when the system asks to use the webcam. Now you are reay to start!
  2. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  3. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
  4. Enjoy the game but remember that the speed of the ball will increase as per the levels
  5. You can click on the restart button to try again if you lose, don't lose hope and try to beat your own score!!
